About

I’m a person who cares a lot about how digital technologies are used to advance the public interest, and who worries a lot about this being done badly.

I’m a former CEO who specialises in helping current leaders to develop clear strategies.

I’m the founder of mySociety, a citizen-empowerment NGO of which I remain hugely proud, running websites like TheyWorkForYou.com and WhatDoTheyKnow.com

I’m a writer on a range of issues relating to power, technology and government.

I’m currently Digital Transformation Lead at the Big Lottery Fund.

I’m a student of economic and technological history.
